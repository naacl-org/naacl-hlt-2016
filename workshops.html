Student Research Workshop

The Student Research Workshop (SRW) provides a venue for student researchers to present their work in computational linguistics and natural language processing. Students receive feedback from the general conference audience as well as from mentors specifically assigned according to the topic of their work. The Student Research Workshop is held as a part of the main conference sessions and attendance is covered within the main conference registration fees.

SRW paper submission deadline is March 11, 2016.
