Information for Presenters


Talk length for long papers: 20 minutes (15 minutes talk + 5 minutes for questions)

Talk length for short papers: 10 minutes (6 minutes talk + 4 minutes for questions)

Poster Size: Poster boards for the poster session will be 8' wide (92 inches/233 cm) and 4' high (46 inches/116 cm). Presenters can print their poster in any size or orientation that fits this board.
