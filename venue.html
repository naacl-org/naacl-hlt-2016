Conference Venue and Hotel

The NAACL HLT 2016 conference hotel is the same as the conference venue, the Sheraton San Diego Resort and Marina. Conference participants receive the following discounted rates:

  • $189/night: single/double room
  • $209/night: triple room
  • $228/night: quad room

All rooms include complimentary internet. Regular attendees can access the site to learn more about the event and to book, modify, or cancel a reservation until Friday, May 20, 2016 (6pm PDT). To make your hotel reservations and take advantage of the Conference Group Rate, please use the following URL:

https://www.starwoodmeeting.com/Book/NAACLHLT2016

Make your booking at the conference hotel through the link below before May 20th, 6pm PDT to receive the discounted conference rate.
